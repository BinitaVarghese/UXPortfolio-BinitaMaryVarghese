MY STORY...

As a UX designer, I enjoy learning how people think and behave differently in similar situations. I combine intensive research with my observation skills and understanding to design experiences that are a little bit more enjoyable and beautiful. My process is hands-on, collaborative and iterative with multiple design refining iterations.

As an Electronics Engineering and a certified PMP professional in the Software industry for more than a decade I have worked on various platforms delivering solutions to problems. These projects have sharpened my skills in problem solving, coding, people management, analytical thinking and critical review.

Relocating multiple times over the past decade has lead me to discover everyday products designed differently and to appreciate the culturally inspired designs. These experiences eventually led me to the world of User Experience and Design. My goal is to design products and experiences that are simple, meaningful, intuitive and impactful.
